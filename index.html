FAQ

How often should I clean my windows?

Most homes are 2–4×/year. Love lots of natural light? Consider monthly or bi-monthly exteriors.

Do I need to be home?

Nope. We can text you updates and photos. We just need access to the yard and a working exterior spigot for pure-water work.

What if it rains?

We include a rain guarantee—if rain affects the work within 14 days, we’ll touch up the affected panes.

Do you handle hard-water spots?

Yes. We use professional acids/polishes as needed and protect surrounding surfaces.
